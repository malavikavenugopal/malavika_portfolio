Market Basket Analysis

Course: Introduction to Business Analytics

Institution: Amrita School of Business, Coimbatore

Tutor: Dr. Prashobhan Palakkeel

Introduction

Market Basket Analysis is a data mining technique used to understand customer purchasing patterns by identifying associations between different products bought together. It helps retailers optimize product placement and design effective cross-selling strategies.

Within business analytics, this technique uses transaction data to uncover hidden relationships between items and predict future buying behavior.

Importance of Market Basket Analysis

  • Identifies product associations to increase sales through targeted promotions.
  • Optimizes store layout and product placement for better customer experience.
  • Enhances inventory management by understanding product demand relationships.
  • Supports personalized marketing by recommending related products.

Common Methods in Market Basket Analysis

  1. Association Rule Learning: Finds rules like "If a customer buys X, they also buy Y."
  2. Support: Measures how frequently items appear together in transactions.
  3. Confidence: Measures the reliability of the association rule.
  4. Lift: Evaluates the strength of a rule compared to random chance.
