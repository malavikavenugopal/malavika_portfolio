Text Mining

Course: Introduction to Business Analytics

Institution: Amrita School of Business, Coimbatore

Tutor: Dr. Prashobhan Palakkeel

Introduction

Text mining, also known as text data mining or text analytics, is the process of extracting meaningful information and patterns from large amounts of unstructured text data. It combines techniques from linguistics, statistics, and machine learning to analyze and interpret textual content.

In business analytics, text mining helps organizations analyze customer feedback, social media posts, and other text sources to gain insights, improve decision-making, and enhance customer experience.

Importance of Text Mining

  • Enables analysis of unstructured data like emails, reviews, and social media.
  • Helps uncover hidden trends, sentiments, and relationships in text.
  • Supports decision-making by providing actionable insights from textual data.
  • Improves customer engagement through sentiment analysis and feedback interpretation.

Common Text Mining Techniques

  1. Tokenization: Breaking text into words or phrases.
  2. Sentiment Analysis: Determining the emotional tone behind text.
  3. Topic Modeling: Identifying topics within large text corpora.
  4. Named Entity Recognition (NER): Extracting names, places, organizations.

Conclusion

Text mining empowers businesses to convert vast amounts of textual information into meaningful knowledge, enabling smarter strategies and better customer relationships.
